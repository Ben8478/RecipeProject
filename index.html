Delicious Brocoli salad

How to create a delicious dish in a dash

Ingredients

  • 500 gram brocoli
  • 1 Onion
  • 200 gram Diced Bacon
  • 250 ml sunflower seeds
  • 250 grams of pitted raisens
    • for the dressing
  • 125 ml lemon juice
  • 15 ml sugar
  • 5ml Celery salt
  • 10 ml mustard powder

Steps

Step 1. Fry 200 grams of diced bacon in a pan until it is crispy. After frying the bacon leave it to cool down in a separate bowl.
Step 2. Cut broccoli into smaller pieces and place it in a mixing bowl.
Step 3. Dice on onion and place in the mixing bowl with the broccoli.
Step 4. Add 250 ml of sunflower seeds to the mix.
Step 5. To make the salad dressing mix together 150 ml of canola oil, 125 ml of lemon juice, 5 ml celery salt, 10 ml mustard powder and 15 ml sugar.
Step 6. Add the bacon and salad dressing to the mix and toss the salad.
Step 7. Leave the mixture in a fridge for 12 hours.
Step 8. Serve the salad chilled with a hand full of raisins.
